ABOUT THE CREATOR

My name is Miguel C. Dias, a 14-year-old Brazilian creator, writer and multimedia artist behind the MIRRORGATE universe.

Before becoming an author, I worked on YouTube projects, graphic design, video editing, game development concepts and experimental internet horror storytelling.

MIRRORGATE was created as a shared universe where completely different stories and tones coexist — from emotional science fiction and surreal comedy to disturbing psychological horror.
