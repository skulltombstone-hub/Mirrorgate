ABOUT MIGUEL

My name is Miguel C. Dias, a 14-year-old Brazilian creator working with books, visual design, editing, storytelling and multimedia fiction.

Before MIRRORGATE, I created YouTube projects, digital art, experimental horror concepts and game development ideas.

MIRRORGATE was created as a fictional universe where stories with completely different tones coexist together — from emotional science fiction to disturbing psychological horror.
